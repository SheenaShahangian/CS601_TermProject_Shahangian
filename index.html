Sheena's Creative Life

This is the story of Sheena, and her unconventional career path in the creative arts, her interests, and what she's up to, in the photography world and beyond.

A Multi-Faceted Career Creative Professional

I believe in living a multi-faceted creative life and career. As a professional photographer, everything I do is about capturing moments. For my everyday work, this means documenting adventurous small weddings (also known as elopements) in the great outdoors for my clients. However, my interests and experience go beyond that. I am also a writer in both the wedding and outdoors spaces, and I document more than just weddings. This includes capturing and selling photos of my travels and the landscapes I come across.

I want this little space on the web to do exactly this: show you the intersections of my life and the complexities of both creating art for myself and for those around me. Being a professional photographer, it's easy to make documentation all about my wedding clients, but it doesn't have to be that way (nor should it!). This portfolio shows you the multi-faceted aspects of who I am both professionally and personally. Who is Sheena? Let's go on a journey to find out.
