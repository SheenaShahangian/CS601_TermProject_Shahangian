In addition to photographing elopements, I've also had some of my writing work published.

Sharing My Experiences and Expertise

As a wedding industry professional, it's my job to know the ins and outs of the industry. It's this skill set that has made me a freelance writer for Green Wedding Shoes, a major US-based wedding blog. I write on all sorts of wedding planning related topics, providing helpful advice and guidance to couples as they put together their dream wedding day.

In addition to my writing for Green Wedding Shoes, I have also submitting my writing to a few other publications, where I've shared my experiences as an elopement photographer, along with my journey as a woman in the great outdoors. This is just a small sample of my writing.
