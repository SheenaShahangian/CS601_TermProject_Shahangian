Finding New Locations for Clients' Elopements

A huge part of why I travel is so that I can find epic destinations for my elopement photography clients. Choosing an incredible place to share your vows is a huge part of the process, and it's my job to help my couples identify those special spaces.

Creating Travel Guides for Adventurers

In the process of traveling to find elopement locations for my couples, I realized that the general public also wanted resources to help them craft their own travel itineraries to the states I frequent. It's because of this need that I've expanded my business offerings to include guides and resources to travelers. Much of my travel calendar focuses on this purpose.
