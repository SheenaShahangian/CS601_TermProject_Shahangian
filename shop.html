Frequently Asked Print Shop Questions

Does the price include sales tax?

No, sales tax will be calculated by the gallery system, based on your specific billing location.

What type of paper is used?

You photograph will be printed on a matte paper to avoid excess shine. It is mounted with a high quality 8 ply mat board.

Is any assembly required?

Nope! All prints are already placed in their frames, so you're good to go! Simply hang it up on your wall and enjoy.

Do you accept returns?

No returns are accepted and refunds will not be issued, unless you have received a damaged product.
